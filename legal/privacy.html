Privacy Policy

Effective date: 11/23/2025

Bus-D Designs ("we", "us", "our") provides app & UI/UX design services. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights.

1. Information We Collect

  • Contact information: name, email, phone, company (collected via contact or onboarding forms).
  • Project details: materials you upload (assets, brand guidelines) and information about your project.
  • Usage data: analytics about how visitors use the website (via third-party services).
  • Cookies: small files stored on your device to improve site experience. See our Cookie Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use information to:

  • Communicate about inquiries, proposals, and projects;
  • Deliver services and provide files and support;
  • Improve our website and services through analytics;
  • Comply with legal obligations and protect our rights.

3. Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell personal data. We may share information with:

  • Service providers (payment processors, hosting, analytics) who process data on our behalf;
  • Developers or collaborators if required for a project's completion (only with your approval if the data is sensitive);
  • When required by law or to protect legal rights.

4. Data Retention & Security

We retain personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes described. We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect information, but no internet transmission is 100% secure.

5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict processing of your personal data. To exercise these rights, contact us at brandon@busddesigns.com.

6. Children

Our services are not directed at children under 13 (or older where local law requires). We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

7. Changes to this Policy

We may update this policy. The effective date above indicates the latest version.
