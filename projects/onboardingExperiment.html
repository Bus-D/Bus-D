Introduction

This is an onboarding process for a hot chocolate company. For the onboarding process it needed to have at least 3 benefits to becoming a member.

Being able to vote on new flavors, having access to free monthly flavors, and early access to the new flavors were the benefits that was determined to be the most wanted.

Design Phase

First Designs

This project didn't end up requiring too many iterations of designs. The main problem with the first set was that the images didn't feel unified. I knew the ones that I chose weren't going to be the final images, but I needed placeholders to get ideas flowing.

Final Design

After getting better images, and tidying up the sizes and layouts, I was happy with the final design.

The design isn't meant to deliver magic, just focusing on value the users want. Free flavors. Early access. Getting a say in what comes next. Benefits like this can help subcriptions grow 8-15% while also keeping people around.
